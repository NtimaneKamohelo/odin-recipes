French Toast Recipe

How to make French Toast

French toast is easy to make, and it can be made just under 5 minutes.

French Toast Ingredients:

  • White/Brown Bread: Depends with prefence of bread you like, both white and bread get the job done.
  • Eggs: When making french toast eggs are essential.
  • Milk: Milk helps add the extra necessary moisture.
  • Vanilla and Cinnamon: The best French toast is warm, cozy, aromatic, and sweet. The cinnamon and vanilla in this recipe are optional, but we definitely recommend using them for the most delicious experience.
  • Salt: Don't skip the salt! Just a pinch won't affect the flavor.
  • Butter: Like so many of the most decadent foods, the best French toast is made with butter.

Detailed Ingredients:

  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • any number of slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly butter/oil a non-sticky pot and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer to the hot pot and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot.
